Delete your deployment

The deployment overview page allows you to delete your deployment.

Deleting is final and cannot be undone. All data is lost. Billing stops immediately when the deployment has been deleted, rounding up to the nearest hour. This means you can easily start a deployment, run some tests, and tear it down again when you are done.

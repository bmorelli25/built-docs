See your billing history

Information about outstanding payments and billing receipts is readily available from the Elasticsearch Service Console.

To see your billing history:

  1. Sign in to the Elasticsearch Service Console.
  2. Go to Account and then Billing History.
  3. Click on the invoice number for a detailed PDF.

See your account activity

Information about your current Elasticsearch Service subscription charges is available directly from the Elasticsearch Service Console and includes:

  • Current usage, per hour
  • Usage so far this month
  • Cluster activity, including the billable hours per cluster

To see your account activity:

  1. Sign in to the Elasticsearch Service Console.
  2. Go to the Account page.

Additional information about your billing history is also available.

Preparing your installation

When planning an Elastic Cloud Enterprise production environment, the following settings must be considered:

  • Hardware
  • Software
  • Networking
  • High availability
  • Separation of roles
  • Capacity planning
  • Load balancers
  • Users
  • JVM heap sizes
  • Wildcard DNS record

Once you have your hosts ready for installation, you can get ECE up and running with Ansible, on many cloud environments, such as AWS, GCP or MS Azure, or on your own hardware.

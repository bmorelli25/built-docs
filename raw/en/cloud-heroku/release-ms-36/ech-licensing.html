Subscription levels

For more information on what is available with different subscription levels, see Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku Subscriptions. You are entitled to use all of the features in Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku that match your subscription level. Please use them to your heart’s content.

If you have a Standard subscription for Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku, you are also entitled to use all Platinum features (except custom  plugins) available on Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku, as long as your deployments are using less than a combined 64GB of memory in  total. This means that you get to enjoy access to features like anomaly detection, field- and document-level  security and graph capabilities.

What happens If I reach 64GB on a Standard subscription?

We sum up the total memory on all of your clusters, and when you exceed a combined total of 64GB RAM, you’ll get a notification that access to Gold, Platinum, and Private security features requires upgrading to a Gold, Platinum, or Private subscription. These features are automatically disabled after a grace period of 45 days, unless you upgrade your subscription or reduce your total RAM usage to 64GB or less.

If you want to continue using those features beyond 64GB RAM total, you should contact us. If you need more time to upgrade your subscription while talking to our Sales team, the grace period can be extended. We’re here to help.

Your upgraded subscription determines which features are available. For example, machine learning and SAML authentication requires a Platinum or Private subscription and is not available if you upgrade to a Gold subscription.

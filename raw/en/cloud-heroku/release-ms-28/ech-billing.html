Your account and billing

Manage your Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku account right from the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console. You can use the Account page to perform the following tasks:

  • Add a credit card to use our service beyond the end of the trial period or to use additional features.
  • Enable multi-factor authentication to add a layer of security to your Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku account.
  • Check your account activity and billing history to see the charges for your subscription.
  • Change the password for your Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku account and update the billing and operational contacts if more than one person from your organization is involved in billing and operations.
  • Stop charges for a deployment by deleting it if you do not want to incur additional costs.
